<p><p>Towel radiators come in two main types: non-thermostatic and thermostatic. Non-thermostatic towel radiators operate at a fixed temperature, providing consistent warmth without user adjustment. They are often simpler and more cost-effective. In contrast, thermostatic towel radiators feature adjustable settings, allowing users to control the temperature for enhanced comfort and energy efficiency. This flexibility makes them popular in modern bathrooms, where precise heating and comfort are prioritized. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and heating needs.</p></p>
