<p><strong>The Steam Turbines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Cycle</li><li>Combined Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam turbines market is primarily divided into two types: steam cycle and combined cycle. Steam cycle turbines utilize steam generated from boiling water to drive the turbine for electricity production, primarily in thermal power plants. In contrast, combined cycle systems integrate both gas and steam turbines, enhancing efficiency by reusing exhaust heat from gas turbines to produce steam for additional power generation. This combination maximizes energy output and efficiency, making it a popular choice in modern power generation.</p></p>
