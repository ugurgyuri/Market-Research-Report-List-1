<p><ul><li>Plain Polyester Film Capacitors</li><li>Metallised Polyester Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyester Film Capacitors market is primarily categorized into two types: Plain Polyester Film Capacitors and Metallised Polyester Film Capacitors. Plain Polyester Film Capacitors are made from thin sheets of polyester film, providing basic capacitance with good stability and insulation properties. In contrast, Metallised Polyester Film Capacitors feature a layer of metal deposited on the polyester film, enhancing capacitance and allowing for a reduced size and improved efficiency. Both types serve various applications in electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
