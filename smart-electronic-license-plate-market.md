<p><p>The Smart Electronic License Plate market primarily encompasses two key components: electronic tags and monitoring base stations (card readers). Electronic tags serve as digital identifiers affixed to vehicles, enabling real-time tracking and data exchange. These tags enhance vehicle management and security. Monitoring base stations, or card readers, facilitate communication with the electronic tags, collecting data for tolling, parking management, and law enforcement. Together, they optimize traffic management, improve revenue systems, and bolster vehicle identification processes.</p></p>
