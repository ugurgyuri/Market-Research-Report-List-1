<p><strong>The Area CCD Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interline Transfer</li><li>Frame Line Transfer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Area CCD Image Sensors market is primarily categorized into Interline Transfer and Frame Transfer types. Interline Transfer sensors incorporate photodiodes and charge storage sites, enabling rapid readout and minimizing motion blur. They're ideal for video applications due to their high-speed capabilities. In contrast, Frame Transfer sensors capture a full frame of data before readout, enhancing image quality but requiring longer exposure times. These types cater to varied applications, from high-speed imaging to scientific and industrial uses, affecting market dynamics significantly.</p></p>
