<p><p>Coin battery holders are essential components in electronic devices, available in two primary market types: surface mount and through hole. Surface mount holders are designed for mounting directly onto the surface of a circuit board, offering a compact and space-efficient solution. In contrast, through hole holders require holes drilled into the circuit board, providing strong mechanical support and robustness. Both types cater to different design requirements and applications, influencing factors such as assembly methods, space constraints, and overall device performance.</p></p>
