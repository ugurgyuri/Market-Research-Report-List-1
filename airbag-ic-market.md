<p><p>The airbag integrated circuit (IC) market is primarily segmented into two types: Integrated System Chips (ISCs) and Independent Chips. Integrated System Chips combine multiple functionalities into a single chip, enhancing efficiency and reducing space requirements, making them ideal for compact automotive designs. On the other hand, Independent Chips function autonomously, providing flexibility for different airbag systems and easier upgrades or replacements. Both types play crucial roles in vehicle safety, catering to diverse automotive manufacturers' needs and technological advancements.</p></p>
