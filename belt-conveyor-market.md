<p><ul><li>Roller</li><li>Spool</li><li>Air Cushion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The belt conveyor market is segmented into three main types: roller, spool, and air cushion conveyors. Roller conveyors utilize a series of cylinders to facilitate movement, ideal for heavy loads. Spool conveyors utilize a spool mechanism for more compact, flexible applications, often suitable for lighter materials. Air cushion conveyors employ a cushion of air to support the belt, reducing friction, making them efficient for delicate or high-speed operations. Each type caters to specific material handling needs across various industries.</p></p>
