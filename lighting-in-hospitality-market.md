<p><p>In the hospitality market, lighting types include CFL (Compact Fluorescent Lamps), LFL (Linear Fluorescent Lamps), HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diodes). CFLs and LFLs are energy-efficient options, providing soft lighting ideal for ambiance. HIDs offer high brightness for larger spaces, but consume more energy. LEDs are the most energy-efficient, long-lasting, and versatile, suitable for various applications from decorative to functional lighting, making them increasingly popular in modern hospitality settings for their cost-effectiveness and sustainability.</p></p>
