<p><p>The motion-activated sprinkler market features two primary types: infrared sensor monitoring and solar motion monitoring. Infrared sensor monitoring utilizes heat detection to identify movement, triggering the sprinkler system to activate and deter animals or intruders. In contrast, solar motion monitoring harnesses solar energy to power the system, making it eco-friendly and cost-effective. Both technologies enhance garden and property security while conserving water, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers seeking efficient pest control and property protection solutions.</p></p>
