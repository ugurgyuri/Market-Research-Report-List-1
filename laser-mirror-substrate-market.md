<p><p>The laser mirror substrate market can be categorized into three main types: flat, convex, and concave mirrors. Flat mirrors are used for precise reflection and are crucial in optical setups. Convex mirrors, with their outward curvature, disperse light, enhancing field of view and imaging applications. Concave mirrors, featuring inward curvature, focus light and are essential in lasers and telescopes. Each type serves distinct functions in optical systems, impacting performance, efficiency, and application across industries like telecommunications and manufacturing.</p></p>
