<p><ul><li>Rotary Cordless Polisher</li><li>Dual Action Cordless Polisher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cordless polisher market includes two main types: rotary and dual action polishers. Rotary cordless polishers offer a direct drive system, providing high-speed rotation ideal for heavy-duty tasks and achieving a high gloss finish. In contrast, dual action cordless polishers combine orbital and rotational movements, making them safer for sensitive surfaces and less likely to cause damage. These types cater to different user needs, from professional detailers to DIY enthusiasts, enhancing versatility in automotive and surface care.</p></p>
