<p><p>The solid wood panel market consists of three primary types: single layer, three layer, and five layer panels. Single layer panels are made from a single piece of wood, offering solid integrity and appearance. Three layer panels feature a core of oriented wood layers, enhancing stability and resistance to warping. Five layer panels use alternating layers, providing superior strength and durability while minimizing shrinkage. Each type caters to different applications, balancing aesthetic appeal with structural performance.</p></p>
