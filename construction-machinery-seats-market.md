<p><ul><li>Mechanical Suspension Seats</li><li>Air Suspension Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction machinery seats market primarily features two types: mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats. Mechanical suspension seats use springs and dampers to absorb shocks and vibrations, providing a stable ride for operators. In contrast, air suspension seats utilize an air-filled bladder system that automatically adjusts to the operator's weight, enhancing comfort and reducing fatigue during long work hours. Both seat types aim to improve operator safety and productivity by minimizing discomfort and enhancing control while navigating rough terrains.</p></p>
