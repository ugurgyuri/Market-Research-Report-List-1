<p><p>The VPN routers market is categorized into wireless and wired types. Wireless VPN routers enable secure remote access to networks wirelessly, offering flexibility and convenience for mobile devices and multiple users. They often support protocols like OpenVPN and IPsec for enhanced security. Conversely, wired VPN routers connect directly through Ethernet cables, providing stable, high-speed connections ideal for stationary setups. Both types ensure encrypted communications, making them essential for businesses and individuals seeking privacy and security in their online activities.</p></p>
