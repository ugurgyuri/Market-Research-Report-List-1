Stern thrusters, located at the rear, assist similarly for improved control during backward navigation. Azimuth thrusters can rotate 360 degrees, providing flexibility in direction and propulsion. Other thruster types encompass specialized solutions for unique vessels or applications, contributing to the overall efficiency and handling capabilities of boats in diverse marine environments.</p></p>
