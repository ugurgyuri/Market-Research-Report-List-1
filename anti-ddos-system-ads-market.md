<p><p>An Anti-DDoS System (ADS) is designed to protect networks from distributed denial-of-service attacks. The market for ADS can be segmented into cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based ADS offers scalability and flexibility, allowing organizations to redirect traffic through external data centers for filtering, making it ideal for businesses with fluctuating traffic volumes. In contrast, on-premise solutions provide organizations with greater control over their systems and data, suitable for those requiring stricter security protocols or with consistent traffic patterns.</p></p>
