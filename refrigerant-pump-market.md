<p><p>The refrigerant pump market features two main types: open type and closed type refrigerant pumps. Open type refrigerant pumps are designed for easy maintenance and can accommodate larger refrigerant systems, typically found in commercial applications. In contrast, closed type refrigerant pumps are sealed units that circulate refrigerant more efficiently and are often used in residential or compact systems. Both types serve the essential function of moving refrigerant in cooling and heating applications, contributing to overall system efficiency and performance.</p></p>
