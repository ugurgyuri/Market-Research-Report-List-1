<p><p>The spiral classifier market can be divided into two main types: High Weir Spiral Classifier and Submerged Spiral Classifier. The High Weir Spiral Classifier features a steep pool and is designed for high-density ore, allowing for efficient separation and classification. In contrast, the Submerged Spiral Classifier operates with a lower pool level, facilitating the handling of more fine materials and providing enhanced accuracy in classification. Both types are essential in mining and mineral processing applications for optimizing material recovery.</p></p>
