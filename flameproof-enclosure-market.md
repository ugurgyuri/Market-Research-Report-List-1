Type I enclosures safeguard against Group I gases (mining applications), while IIA is designed for gases like propane, and IIB for gases like ethylene. Type IIC offers the highest protection, suitable for the most hazardous gases such as hydrogen and acetylene. Each type reflects increasing levels of risk, dictating design standards and materials used to ensure safety in explosive atmospheres.</p></p>
