<p><p>The Medical Grade EMI Filter market is categorized into two primary types: Single Phase EMI Filters and Three Phase EMI Filters. Single Phase EMI Filters are designed for devices that operate on a single phase of power, providing efficient electromagnetic interference attenuation for medical equipment. In contrast, Three Phase EMI Filters cater to more complex systems, managing interference in multi-phase electrical networks commonly used in larger medical facilities. Both filter types ensure compliance with stringent regulations, safeguarding sensitive medical devices from EMI.</p></p>
