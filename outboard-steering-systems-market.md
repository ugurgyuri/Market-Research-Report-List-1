<p><strong>The Outboard Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Power Steering</li><li>Hydraulic Steering</li><li>Mechanical Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outboard steering systems market encompasses three main types: Electronic Power Steering (EPS), Hydraulic Steering, and Mechanical Steering. EPS utilizes electric motors for precision and responsiveness, enhancing fuel efficiency and reducing weight. Hydraulic Steering relies on hydraulic fluid to provide smooth steering feedback and is favored for larger vessels due to its robust performance. Mechanical Steering uses physical linkages for a direct connection between the helm and the outboard, offering simplicity and reliability, particularly in smaller boats.</p></p>
