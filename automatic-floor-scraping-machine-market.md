<p><ul><li>Walk-Behind Machine</li><li>Ride-on Machine</li><li>Self-Propelled Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Floor Scraping Machine market comprises three primary types: Walk-Behind Machines, Ride-On Machines, and Self-Propelled Machines. Walk-Behind Machines require the operator to manually steer them, ideal for smaller spaces. Ride-On Machines allow operators to sit while cleaning, suitable for larger areas for increased efficiency. Self-Propelled Machines offer autonomous navigation, enhancing productivity by minimizing operator involvement. Each type caters to different operational needs, making them essential in various commercial and industrial cleaning environments.</p></p>
