<p><p>Stretch wrapping machines are categorized into three main types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic machines streamline the wrapping process, requiring minimal human intervention, ideal for high-volume operations. Semi-automatic machines offer a balance between efficiency and manual control, where operators assist in wrapping while the machine takes care of film application. Manual machines rely entirely on human effort, making them suitable for low-volume or flexible operations but require more labor and time commitment.</p></p>
