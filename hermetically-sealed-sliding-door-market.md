<p><strong>The Hermetically Sealed Sliding Door Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Leaf</li><li>Double Leaf</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hermetically sealed sliding doors are categorized primarily into single leaf and double leaf types. Single leaf doors consist of one panel that slides, ideal for spaces with limited openings and minimal traffic. In contrast, double leaf doors feature two panels that slide to create a wider entry, accommodating higher foot traffic and larger equipment. Both types offer superior sealing to ensure containment of air, particles, and contaminants, making them essential in industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing where hygiene and safety are critical.</p></p>
