<p><p>Energy Performance Contracting (EPC) is a financing mechanism that enables organizations to improve energy efficiency and reduce operational costs without upfront capital investment. Under an EPC, a service provider conducts an energy audit, implements energy-saving measures, and guarantees savings over a defined period. The savings generated typically cover the costs of implementing the solutions, allowing clients to invest in sustainable practices while minimizing financial risk.</p><p>The EPC market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for energy efficiency and sustainability. Organizations across various sectors are focusing on reducing their carbon footprints and energy costs, spurred by regulatory mandates and consumer preferences for greener practices.
