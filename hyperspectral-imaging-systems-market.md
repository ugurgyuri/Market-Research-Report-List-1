Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) focuses on wavelengths between VNIR and MWIR, useful for detecting moisture and chemical compositions.</p><p>3. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) is suited for thermal imaging and defense applications.</p><p>4. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) targets thermal emissions, widely used in building inspections and surveillance.</p></p>
