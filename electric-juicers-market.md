<p><p>The electric juicers market consists of three main types: centrifugal, masticating, and others. Centrifugal juicers use high-speed blades to shred fruits and vegetables, extracting juice quickly but generating heat, which can affect nutrient retention. Masticating juicers operate at lower speeds, grinding produce to maximize juice extraction and preserve nutrients. The "other" category includes specialized juicers like triturating and manual options, catering to niche preferences and offering varied functionalities for diverse consumer needs in juice preparation.</p></p>
