<p><p>The mass spectrometer market features various types, including GC-MS, which combines gas chromatography for volatile samples with mass spectrometry for identification. LC-MS uses liquid chromatography for analyzing complex mixtures, particularly in bioanalytical applications. MALDI-TOF focuses on large biomolecules and polymers, utilizing laser ionization. ICP-MS is tailored for elemental analysis, employing plasma to ionize samples. Other types encompass diverse techniques for specialized applications in research, clinical labs, and industrial settings, enhancing analytical capabilities across multiple fields.</p></p>
