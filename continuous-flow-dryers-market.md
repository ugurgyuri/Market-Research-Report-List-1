<p><ul><li>Tower</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous flow dryers are essential in various industrial applications for efficiently drying bulk materials. The tower dryer operates in a vertical configuration, allowing materials to move downward while hot air flows upward, enhancing contact and maximizing heat exchange. In contrast, horizontal dryers feature a parallel arrangement where materials progress along a conveyor while being exposed to heated air. Both types cater to different processing needs and capacities, with tower dryers suited for high-efficiency applications and horizontal dryers offering versatility in product handling.</p></p>
