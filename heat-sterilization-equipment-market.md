<p><ul><li>Dry Heat Sterilization Equipment</li><li>Moist Heat Sterilization Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat sterilization equipment market is divided into two main types: dry heat sterilization equipment and moist heat sterilization equipment. Dry heat sterilization employs high temperatures to kill microorganisms and is ideal for heat-stable items like glassware and metal instruments, providing a residue-free process. Moist heat sterilization, commonly using steam under pressure (autoclaving), is effective for a broader range of materials, including surgical instruments and textiles, as it penetrates and eliminates microorganisms efficiently through heat and moisture.</p></p>
