<p><p>Display advertising software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering scalability, flexibility, and ease of updates. They allow users to manage campaigns from anywhere, promoting collaboration. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a company's servers, providing more control over data and security. However, it requires significant IT resources for maintenance and upgrades, making it less flexible than cloud-based options.</p></p>
