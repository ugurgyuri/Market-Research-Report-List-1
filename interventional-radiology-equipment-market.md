<p><p>The Interventional Radiology Equipment Market includes various imaging systems critical for minimally invasive procedures. MRI systems utilize magnetic fields for detailed internal imaging, while Ultrasound Imaging Systems use sound waves for real-time visualization. CT Scanners provide cross-sectional images for precise diagnosis. Angiography Systems visualize blood vessels, aiding in vascular interventions. Fluoroscopy Systems offer continuous X-ray imaging for guidance during procedures, and Biopsy Systems assist in obtaining tissue samples for analysis. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing patient care and procedural accuracy.</p></p>
