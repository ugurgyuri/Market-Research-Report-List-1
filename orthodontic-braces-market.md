<p><p>The orthodontic braces market consists of various types, each catering to different patient needs. Traditional braces, made of metal, are durable and effective for severe alignment issues. Ceramic braces offer a more aesthetic option with tooth-colored materials, blending better with teeth. Clear aligners provide a removable, nearly invisible alternative for mild to moderate corrections. Lingual braces are placed on the back of the teeth, making them discreet, ideal for those seeking a hidden solution. Each type plays a distinct role in orthodontic treatment.</p></p>
