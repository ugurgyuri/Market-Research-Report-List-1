<p><ul><li>Single Joint</li><li>Multiple Joint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gait rehabilitation exoskeletons are categorized into single joint and multiple joint types based on the range of movement they support. Single joint exoskeletons are designed to assist or rehabilitate one specific joint, typically targeting localized issues and improving mobility at that joint. In contrast, multiple joint exoskeletons engage several joints simultaneously, offering a more comprehensive approach to gait rehabilitation. This broader functionality is beneficial for individuals with more complex movement challenges, enhancing overall mobility and coordination during therapy.</p></p>
