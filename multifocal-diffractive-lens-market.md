<p><p>The multifocal diffractive lens market consists primarily of diffractive bifocal and trifocal lenses. Diffractive bifocal lenses feature two distinct focal points, enabling clear vision at both near and far distances, ideal for individuals with presbyopia. In contrast, diffractive trifocal lenses offer three focal points, accommodating near, intermediate, and distance vision, enhancing versatility for daily activities. Both lens types utilize diffraction technology to effectively manage light, providing improved visual clarity and comfort for users across varying distances.</p></p>
