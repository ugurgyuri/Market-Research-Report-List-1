<p><p>The hand grease gun market is primarily categorized into two types: sharp mouth and flat mouth grease guns. Sharp mouth grease guns feature a pointed nozzle, allowing for precision application of grease in tight or hard-to-reach areas, making them ideal for intricate machinery and automotive use. In contrast, flat mouth grease guns are designed with a broader, flat nozzle, facilitating grease distribution over larger surfaces or components, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications like industrial equipment and large vehicles.</p></p>
