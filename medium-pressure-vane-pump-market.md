<p><p>Medium pressure vane pumps are classified into two main types: fixed displacement and variable displacement. Fixed displacement pumps deliver a constant flow rate regardless of the system pressure, making them suitable for applications requiring steady fluid transfer. In contrast, variable displacement pumps adjust their output flow based on system demands, providing efficiency and flexibility for varying operational conditions. This adaptability enables them to better handle fluctuating loads and pressures, making them ideal for dynamic applications in various industries.</p></p>
