<p><p>The dish drying rack market is divided into two primary types based on installation: countertop and sink installation. Countertop drying racks are freestanding units that sit on kitchen counters, providing flexibility and convenience while draining excess water into a tray. In contrast, sink installation racks are designed to fit over or alongside kitchen sinks, maximizing space efficiency and facilitating direct drainage into the sink. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, enhancing kitchen organization and drying efficiency.</p></p>
